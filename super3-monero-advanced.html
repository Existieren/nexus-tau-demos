The State Explosion Problem

The Problem

1018+

A privacy system with 150M outputs, temporal state across thousands of blocks, and multiple interacting layers has more states than atoms in a grain of sand. Brute-force checking is physically impossible.

How Tau Solves It

BDD

Binary Decision Diagrams compress the state space by exploiting structure in the rules. Tau does not enumerate states -- it checks whether the specification's logic permits temporal leaks. Billions of states collapse into a compact graph.

The Analogy

H₂O

You do not need to taste every drop of water to know its chemical formula. Tau reads the formula (specification) and proves properties about every possible molecule (system state). The formula is small; the ocean of states is infinite.
